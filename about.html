I have a B.S. in Mechatronics Engineering from Universidad Nacional de Ingenieria, one of the best universities in Peru. However, I not only want to tell you about my professional life, but also about my others facets.

MUSIC GROUP AT SCHOOL

Yes, although many whose meet me since my universities years have not belived it, I participated in the student group of folkloric music, of course from Juliaca. I invite you to take pleasure hearing this mix of that musical group conformed by primary and secondary school students. youtube music

MOVIE “EL HUERFANITO”

Having being 10 years old, and having passed a casting for a film, I played the character called Luchito, a poor boy from the Juliaca city. The movie shows how to face familiar violence and some society’s injustices. In addition, you can watch the traditional carnival in my native city, the “Candelaria” Carnival. It is held on February every year, you will not regret visiting it someday. Thanks to this experience, I could visited many cities in Peru like Lima because it was the first film of the southern Peruvian region to premiere in Lima cinemas. Movie trailer here

INTERNSHIP IN BRAZIL

I started my university and then an important internship in Brazil. I worked in programming and simulation of trajectory control of an electrical vehicle at Integrated System Department at UNICAMP , where I am supervised by PhD. Ely Paiva.

I enjoy meeting new friends not only from Brazil, but great colleagues from Spain, Peru and Switzerland. I will never forget the “Churrascos” in “Republicas” (big homes with housemates), the Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro and waterfalls of Iguazu.

PAPER ON MUSME

My first submitted paper to an international symposium! As part of the research project about improving fatal accident rates of underground mining workers using robotics technology and processing of sensor data, we publish the results on: International Symposium on Multibody Systems and Mechatronics held in Huatulco, Mexico. Links: Springer, Google books.

I am very grateful to FINCyT PERU and UNI for their financial support.

INNOVATION AWARD by SNMPE

1st Place - Mining & Energy Hackathon - May 2016. Institution: National Society of Mining, Oil and Energy(SNMPE) and ESAN University. Description: Innovative Technology in Social Responsibility for the Energy and Mining Sector. Publish on magazine: Desde Adentro. On RPP Blog.

SILICON VALLEY EEUU

In September 2015, me and university classmates participated in the international contest of student satellites ARLISS representing Peru with groups from other countries like USA, Egypt, Costa Rica and Japan. Unfortunately, we could not win but we have been mentoring a new group of students to participate in this challenge (more information or if you are interested on it, visit Smart Machines Lab. ). So, I could visited the famous east side of USA, specifically the Google offices, Apple and, thanks to Renato Salas and Robert Wang, offices of Oculus in Menlo Park. By the way, Renato Salas is an UNI professional I admire. He cofounded his start-up, Surreal Vision, and it was acquired by Oculus. (Facebook had acquired Oculus on March, 2014). +info here
